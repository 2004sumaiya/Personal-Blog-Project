Trust the Writer of Your Story

Category: Faith & Reflection | Posted on 27 Sep 2025

The future that you're worried about—
You might not even be a part of it.
So why lose sleep over days not promised?
Why fear a tomorrow you may never meet?

So don’t waste your heart on fear.
Fear is heavy. Tawakkul (trust in Allah) is light.
And no heart that leans on Allah ever breaks in vain.
Some days will test you.

Some nights will feel endless.
But know this:
Your Rabb never sleeps, never forgets, and never leaves.
Cry to Him, not to the world.

Plan, but submit.
Dream, but don’t cling.
Because what’s written for you—will find you.
And what isn’t—will miss you, even if it’s inches away.
