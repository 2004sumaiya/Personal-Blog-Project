Social Media & Comparison – The Silent Destroyer of Peace

Category: Mindful Living | Posted on 27 Sep 2025

In a world filled with endless scrolling, perfect pictures, and curated lives, it's easy to fall into the trap of comparison.

One moment you're content, and the next, you're questioning your worth because someone else's life seems "better."

Social media often portrays highlights, not reality.

The perfect smiles may hide struggles, and the flawless skin may mask insecurities.

Comparing your behind-the-scenes life to someone's polished display can silently steal your peace.

You scroll, you see, you compare, you sink.

Our beloved Prophet (ﷺ) advised us, "Look at those below you (in wealth and worldly matters) and not those above you, as it is more suitable to remember the blessings of Allah upon you." (Reference: Sunan Ibn Majah 4142)

The world everyone else is chasing…

And the truth your soul needs to listen to…

“The life of this world is just play and amusement… it will fade like crops after the rain.” (Qur’an 57:20)
