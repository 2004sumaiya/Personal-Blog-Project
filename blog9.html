Learn Arabic Language

Category: Learning Arabic | Posted on 7 Oct 2025

The greatest blessing Allah has bestowed upon this ummah is The Quran. And The Quran was revealed in a language chosen by Allah Himself, The Arabic Language.

Allah says, "Indeed, We have sent it down as an Arabic Quran so that you may understand."
[Surah Yusuf 12:2]

This is not just any language.
This is the tongue in which Jibreel descended from the heavens, carrying the words of Allah to the heart of Muhammad صَلَّى اللّٰهُ عَلَيْهِ وَسَلَّمَ .

It is so sad , the Quran is recited daily in our prayers, yet our heart does not taste it's sweetness.

Ibn Taymiyyah (رَحِمَهُ ٱللَّٰهُ) said:"The Arabic Language is part of the religion, and knowing it is obligatory. For understanding the Quran and Sunnah is not possible without it."

Learning Arabic is not only for scholars. It is for every Muslim who wishes to taste the Qur'an with his heart, not just his ears.

When we sincerely begin to learn this beautiful language, Allah will definitely open doors for us.

Let us make a promise today. Just as we strive to earn for our dunya, let us also strive to earn the language of our deen.

Even if it takes years, every moment spent is ibadah.

This language carries a beauty, a power, and a depth that no other language can hold.

Everything beautiful requires effort.

It is a key to our deen. When we will learn it, we will feel as if a door has opened.

Life is slipping away. Days become months. Months become years. And the Qur'an waits, calling us to understand it directly.

May Allah grant us sincerity in learning this language.
Aameen
