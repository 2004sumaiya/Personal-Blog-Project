Trust Allah

Category: Faith & Reflection | Posted on 27 Sep 2025

There are no sad endings for those who trust Allah.
Because every trial is a hidden mercy,
Every delay is a divine plan,
Every loss is a path to something better,
And every unanswered prayer is a blessing in disguise.

He hears the whispers of your heart
When no one else understands your silence.
He sees the tears that never fall,
And replaces them with peace that the world can’t give.

When the world feels heavy,
And your soul feels tired,
Know that Allah’s wisdom is deeper than your pain.
He never abandons the hearts that remember Him.
So stay patient, stay hopeful.

Even if it hurts now,
Even if the journey seems endless—
With Allah, no struggle is wasted,
No wound is permanent,
And no ending is ever sad—
Only a new beginning in His perfect plan.
